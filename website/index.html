Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

ScoreKeep ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

ScoreKeep collects minimal data to improve app quality:

  • Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics): We collect anonymous usage data such as app launches, screen views, and feature usage. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics): We automatically collect crash logs and performance data to identify and fix bugs. These reports include device type, OS version, and app state at the time of the crash, but do not contain personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not collect payment or financial information.

Local Data Storage

All game sessions, player names, and scores are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics — for anonymous usage analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — for crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy

We do not share your data with any other third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

ScoreKeep does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.
